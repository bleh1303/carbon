About Us

Digital activity is emerging as the next frontier in the sustainability movement. And we as a team plan to create awareness amongst the users about how our online(digital) activities affect the climate and how we can create a sense of self awareness about this.Our aim is help you reducing your carbon footprint which can indeed can help you live a healthier lifestyle, as well as save you money. Whether it's cleaner air, a healthier diet, or reduced energy bills, these benefits of reducing your carbon footprint also mean you're doing your bit to combat climate change.
